Things you'd want to know about instructional design :)

Instructional design isn't JUST about going digital.

It's more about building up 21st century skills for the generation of digital habitats.
Using IT in education, enables students to "play and fight" on their home streets.

BUT a lesson won't be engaging without...

considerations on
​…​
​ science of learning

Instructional designers collabrates appropriate IT techniques with the science of learning, looking for effective approaches that interpret curriculum information to faciliate learning process and create blended learning environments for active knowledge transmission.

"Digital Instructional Lesson > A Sage On Stage"
No

The traditional teacher's role as an information transporter is now superseded by digital learning resources in this blended-learning approach. It could reduce teachers' workload by releasing them from the passive knowledge transmission process, meanwhile, the big data from the digital resource enables teachers to facilitate students' learning more individual-focused and personalized. They can even achieve the irreplaceable role as the "soul-engineers".
